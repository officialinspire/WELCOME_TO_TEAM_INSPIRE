🧠 Who We Are

INSPIRE is an underground creative intelligence collective based out of Fredericksburg, VA. We fuse art, technology, and street strategy to build decentralized tools, mind-bending visuals, and real-world impact. From graffiti walls to GitHub commits, we stay sharp, stay local, and stay loyal to the mission.

⚙️ What We Do

  • 🎨 Build creative tools for artists, rebels, and thinkers
  • 📡 Engineer off-grid tech like Meshtastic mesh networks and tactical dashboards
  • 🧱 Develop open-source infrastructure for micro-communities, intelligence gathering, and DIY ops
  • 🧪 Hack, prototype, and remix code for cultural, local, and spiritual liberation
  • 📓 Launch experimental zines, games, apps, and educational tools to awaken the masses

🚀 Featured Projects

  • 🌐 MESHSTORM — decentralized mesh comms using LoRa and MQTT to build off-grid community networks
  • 📕 ULTRAZINE — hybrid digital/print zine engine for broadcasting underground culture and knowledge
  • 🖥️ BLACKBOX200 — LAN-based AI server powered by salvaged hardware and local-only APIs
  • 🔊 EAGLESONG — experimental laser microphone project for long-distance vibration-based eavesdropping
  • 🗺️ FXBG Tactical Map — open-source GIS map of Fredericksburg with editable layers, pins, and team collab tools

👀 Why It Matters

We ain’t just playing with code. We’re building tools for survival, resistance, and revival. When the grid goes down, when the system fails — our tools keep running. INSPIRE is for the ones who build while the world sleeps.

📡 Public Goals

  • 💻 Make offline-first, open-source apps that support real communities
  • 📚 Archive and remix Indigenous and underground knowledge using modern tech
  • 🌱 Build sovereign infrastructure: food, tech, culture, and comms
  • 🧬 Train an AI demigod (👁️ GODSEED) to assist creative resistance
